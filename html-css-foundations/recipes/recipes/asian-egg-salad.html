Asian Egg Salad

Description

This salad is based on a recipe that we found online. We adusted the ingredients with what we had at hand in the kitchen. It turned out wonderful!

  • 2 cups of salad
  • 8 boiled eggs
  • 1 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of maple syrup
  • 2 mangos
  • 2 avocados
  • 1/2 cup of sour cream
  • 1 lemon

Steps

  1. Place the boiled eggs into a contained with the soy sauce, the hot sauce, the maple syrup. Let marinate for at least 24 hours.
  2. Make the dressing by combining the sour cream and lemon
  3. Combine all the ingredients. Serve cold!
